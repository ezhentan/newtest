About Me

My name is E-Zhen Tan, a Year 3 undergraduate at the Singapore University of Social Sciences. As an aspiring data scientist, I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Business Analytics with Minor in Algorithmic Development. I love discovering the versatility of code, from using Python to create dictionaries (actual ones, not the data type '{}') to creating websites with R.

From the past semesters of university, I have learnt how to read and analyse data using Python and IBM SPSS Modeler. In the Python Programming module, I learnt how to import data from text, json and csv files and use the pandas and matplotlib libraries to create dataframes and plot graphs for data visualization. My most recent internship as a Data Science Intern has deepened my knowledge and given me real-world experience in using Python for machine learning.

In addition to Python, I am able to create visuals and graphs of present and historical data using Tableau. Equipped with these skills, I am able to manage data and assist in giving meaning to the vast sea of data available. My portfolio of prior school projects are linked here and in the tab, GitHub, above.

A self-directed learner at heart, I have pushed myself to learn other codes beyond what is taught in the school curriculum. In building this website using R, I have also used the HTML language to format the text.

Apart from these technical skills, my part-time job as a tutor at Writers@Work Enrichment, a local tuition centre that specialises in teaching English Language for Primary and Secondary levels, has also helped me to hone my communication skills. Be it in writing when I am marking scripts or speaking when teaching a class, it is important that my language is sufficiently clear to ensure that the students will be able to understand the lesson and their mistakes.
